WHAT INSPIRES US

The major vision of the "Bio-Assistant" is to reduce the health-care expenditure of consumers and hospitals through the application of novel telemetry hardware and AI technology

    The primary focus of the Bio-Assistant lies in the technological innovations in the following areas:
    1. Automated multifunctional medical device with smaller assessment cycle to reduce medical practitioner's efforts and time expenditure on redundant manual tasks

    2. Non-invasive wearable clinical monitors for tracking "continuous blood pressure, blood glucose level and other vital information" with an automated wellness management and therapy recommendation system

    3. An economical daily health management wearable device with interactive and attractive low powered design

    4. Health Disorder prevention and coherence prediction database
